Install the app

This site is a progressive web app (PWA): you can add it to your phone or computer like a regular app — no app store needed. Installing gives you an icon on your home screen and, on iPhone and iPad, it's required for push notifications to work.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen," then Add.
  4. Open the app from the new home-screen icon, then turn on push notifications from the sign-up or manage page.

Apple only allows web push for installed apps (iOS 16.4 or newer). Push enabled in a regular Safari tab won't work on iPhone/iPad.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open this site in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu → "Install app" (or "Add to Home screen").

Optional — push notifications also work in the regular browser on Android.

Computer (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Look for the install icon at the right end of the address bar (a screen with a down arrow) and click it.

Optional — push notifications also work in the regular browser on desktop (Chrome, Edge, and Firefox).

Do I need to install it?

Only if you want push notifications on an iPhone or iPad. Everywhere else the site works the same installed or not, and email alerts never require the app.
