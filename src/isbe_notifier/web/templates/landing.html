Follow the money in Illinois elections

This is a free notification service that watches the Illinois State Board of Elections' public feed around the clock. The moment a campaign committee reports a major contribution, an independent expenditure, or any other disclosure, we send an alert by email or push notification — with every contribution and expenditure itemized.

It was built for the 2026 Chicago Public Schools Board races (the citywide president's seat and all 20 district seats), but you can also follow any Illinois committee — or every filing statewide.

How it works

  • Real-time alerts — email and/or push notifications minutes after a report appears, including every itemized contribution and expenditure.
  • Daily & weekly summaries — optional digest emails that collect everything you follow in one message.
  • Race matching — independent expenditures are matched to CPS races by the "Office – District" they target, no matter which committee spends the money.

Every alert links back to the original report on elections.il.gov. Unsubscribe anytime, from any email.
